A BRIEF BIOGRAPHY OF TB JOSHUA


BORN ON JUNE 12, 1963 - DIED ON JUNE 5, 2021

Temitope Balogun Joshua, widely known as TB Joshua, was a Nigerian pastor, televangelist, and philanthropist. Born on June 12, 1963, in Arigidi Akoko, Ondo State, Nigeria, he founded the Synagogue Church of All Nations (SCOAN) in 1987, which became one of the most influential Christian ministries in Africa . He was also the founder of Emmanuel TV, a Christian television station that gained a global audience through its broadcasts of healing services and teachings. TB Joshua was known for his charismatic preaching and claims of performing miracles, including healings and prophecies. His ministry attracted followers from around the world, and he was recognized for his humanitarian efforts, such as providing scholarships, disaster relief, and support for the less privileged .
